As a matter of fact, all of his creation or been part of his career also became my favorite, but there is one that I have been watched for many times entitled Princess Mononoke. This film is all about a human girl that adopted by the pack of wolves due to decision of leaving the child by her parents and then acquire of willingness to sacrifice her life as became part of the nature. She is very persistent and brave to fight to her own species against the abusive traits of human behavior towards the forest and gods. Also there’s this boy who went along from the far north east part of indigenous people to travel all the way to the west to cure his cursed arms. The boy has the spirit of protecting both human and nature; he helps who he would see. Together, they both tried to save the worlds from its sudden destruction on the domineering of human. Basically the conflict to the story is a man versus the nature. One side keeps dominating, and thus how this reflects in our real situation, and human.
